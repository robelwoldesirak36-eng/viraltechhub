How to Avoid Online Scams and Fraud

Online scams are increasing worldwide. Awareness is the best protection.

Never Share OTP Codes

No legitimate company will request your OTP via phone or email.

Check Website URLs

Always verify the domain before entering personal information.

Beware of Too-Good-To-Be-True Offers

Fraudsters often use unrealistic rewards to trap victims.

Conclusion

Careful browsing habits protect your finances and identity.
