Hidden Android Battery Saving Secrets

Battery draining too fast? Most Android users don’t know that powerful battery-saving settings already exist inside their phones. You don’t need third-party apps. You just need to adjust the right system settings.

1. Turn Off Background App Refresh

Go to Settings → Apps → Battery → Background usage limits. Restrict apps you don’t use often.

2. Disable Always-On Display

This feature consumes battery constantly. Turning it off can save 5–10% daily.

3. Use Dark Mode

If you have AMOLED screen, dark mode saves real battery power.

4. Reduce Screen Timeout

Set screen timeout to 30 seconds or 1 minute.

5. Turn Off Unused Sensors

Disable Bluetooth, GPS, and NFC when not needed.

Conclusion

These simple settings can increase battery life by 20–40% without installing anything.
