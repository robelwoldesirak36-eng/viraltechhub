Hidden Android Battery Saving Secrets

If your phone battery drains fast, these tips will help.

1. Limit Background Apps

Go to settings and restrict apps running in the background.

2. Reduce Screen Refresh Rate

Switch from 120Hz to 60Hz to save power.

3. Use Dark Mode

OLED screens consume less power in dark mode.

4. Turn Off Auto-Sync

Disable unnecessary syncing apps.

These simple tricks can extend battery life significantly.
