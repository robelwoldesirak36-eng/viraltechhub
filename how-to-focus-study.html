How to Improve Focus While Studying

Maintaining focus is one of the biggest challenges students face today. With the right methods, concentration can be improved significantly.

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a short break.

Remove Digital Distractions

Turn off notifications while studying.

Create Study Routine

Studying at the same time daily improves mental discipline.

Conclusion

Consistency and environment control are key to strong concentration.
