Cybersecurity Basics Everyone Should Know in 2026

Cyber threats are increasing every year. Protecting your digital life is now essential.

Use Strong Passwords

Always use long passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

2FA adds an extra layer of security to your accounts.

Avoid Public Wi-Fi Risks

Public Wi-Fi networks are often insecure. Use a VPN when necessary.

Keep Software Updated

Updates fix security vulnerabilities that hackers exploit.
